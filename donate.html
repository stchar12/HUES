Donate to HUES!

HUES is always looking for people to donate so
we can implement new and exciting ways to give
back to the LGBTQ community. All donations are funneled
directly to LAHR and are then distributed to us.

Your Donations Will...

Sponsor Events

Your donation can help us plan and host large social and educational events like The Come Out, giving us the resources necessary to book venues, host speakers and other entertainment, and create event booklets and resources.

Create New Programs

Your donation can help us begin new HUES initiatives and programs, such as a monthly newsletter or an educational Youtube video series.

Produce Educational Materials

Your donation can help us create and distribute educational materials that we can use to foster identity exploration within the LGBTQ community, as well as learning and acceptance for those outside the LGBTQ community.
