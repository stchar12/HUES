Alysa Hodgson

Chair

Alysa Hodgson is graduating in May 2016 from MSU with a degree in Women’s and Gender Studies with a minor in LGBTQ & Sexuality Studies. She serves on various community and campus boards and works for the local Democratic Party and the Sexual Assault and Relationship Violence Prevention Program at MSU. She is the mommy to 3 kitties and has been happily het-partnered for over 5 years.

Marissa Connell

Vice Chair

Marissa Connell will be graduating from Michigan State University in May 2016 with a double major in Psychology and Women's and Gender Studies and a minor in LGBTQ and Sexuality Studies. Following graduation, she will be pursuing a Masters degree in Women's and Gender Studies at Eastern Michigan University. Marissa enjoys loud music, watching Netflix, and eating way too much Chipotle. She has hopes of one day becoming a mom to a cuddly Himalayan cat.

Treasurer

She would like to go to grad school and eventually find a career with a focus on activism and education as well as writing. She likes to spend her free time writing and watching Netflix, and is obsessed with lipstick, queer playlists, and hummus.

Katie Trudell

Ambassador

Katie Trudell is 24 years old. She works full time in life insurance and it the Ambassador for HUES. She plans on going back to school for Gender Studies with a minor in Human Resources. Her ideal job and goal is to help the LGBT* community in large corporations and businesses.

In her free time she loves to sing and is part of a women’s chorus called Sistrum. She also loves reading and watching Netflix with her cat named River. Their favorite show to watch together is Once Upon a Time.
