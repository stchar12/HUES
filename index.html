HUES

HUES (Humans Uniting for an Equal Society) is an auxiliary
nonprofit organization of LAHR (Lansing Association for Human
Rights). HUES is dedicated to serving and advocating for the
LGBTQ community in Lansing, Michigan.

Education

Through educational events, presentations, and monthly discussions, HUES aims to create conversations around the major issues surrounding the LGBTQ community today, and emphasizes giving all marginalized identities a chance to both speak out and be listened to.

Leadership

HUES, through mentorship and community building, aims to foster leadership within young adults in the LGBTQ community, giving people the chance to take on leadership roles in the organization and to become connected with leadership in other organizations in the area.

Community

Through social events and community outreach, HUES aspires to bring together LGBTQ people in Lansing, giving people a place where they can feel they belong with others that have lived experiences similar to theirs.
